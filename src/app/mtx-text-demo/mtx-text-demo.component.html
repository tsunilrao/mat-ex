Mtx Text (MtxTextModule) Mtx-Text is a way of arranging type to make text legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. Mtx-Text is based on the guidelines from the Material Design spec and is arranged into multiple tags. Each tag has a font-size, line-height and font-weight.
